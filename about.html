About Me

Hello! I'm Jonathan Yabes.



Proud father of two, husband to a lovely wife, and dedicated family man.

With a whopping 18 years in the industry, my heart beats for weaving tales through motion design, videography, and the magic of video editing. Picture this: from dreaming up concepts to injecting life into scenes with animation, it's my gig! I'm on a mission to not just tell stories but to make you feel them.

When I'm not crafting visual wonders, catch me outdoors soaking in nature or creating unforgettable moments with my family.

Join me on this rollercoaster ride of creativity! My journey as a Video Producer has seen its share of awards and nods, all thanks to the countless hours I've poured into motion design, precise video editing, and capturing the perfect shot. From heart-touching films to snappy promotional videos, my commitment to top-notch quality and creativity has left a trail of satisfied clients and international recognition.

Ready to make your moments unforgettable? If you're on the lookout for a dynamic partner in motion design, videography, video editing, and video production, let's chat! Drop me a line, and let's whip up something extraordinary together!
